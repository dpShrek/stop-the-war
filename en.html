About us

We, the inhabitants of the Dnipro, with a great desire to help the victims of the WAR and our defenders at a difficult time for Ukraine!
Observing the work of the funds to which we also donated funds, were safely stolen and spent in an unknown direction.
We have decided to organize our own aid fund, we will purchase provisions and deliver them directly to the needy!

Fundraising

We have opened an account to which everyone who cares can send funds and track their use.

Purchase

We will personally search and purchase all goods search for reliable suppliers with adequate prices!

Delivery

We will also deliver all purchased goods to the victims and our brave defenders of the Armed Forces!

Our goal

Help as many people as possible, personally control everything and win this WAR!

Food

We raise funds for food for migrants, as well as for the supply of supplies to checkpoints and military units.

Clothes

We raise funds for clothing for migrants, clothing and ammunition for soldiers.

Armament

We raise funds for bulletproof vests, night vision devices and cars.
